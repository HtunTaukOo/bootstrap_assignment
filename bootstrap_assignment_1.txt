col-lg-6
 Columns will stack on both mobile AND tablet screens, only displaying side by side on laptop/desktop screens

col-sm-6
 Columns will display side by side on almost all devices except very small phones

Brief Analysis

col-sm-6: Most aggressive - columns stay side by side on most screens
col-md-6: Balanced approach - stacks on mobile, side by side on tablets+
col-lg-6: Most conservative - stacks on mobile and tablets, side by side only on larger screens

 The choice depends on your content and target audience. For a product table with descriptions, col-md-6 is often the best balance between readability and space utilization.
